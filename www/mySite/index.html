Free Agency Roundup: Markieff Morris staying with Lakers

The veteran says he is ready to "run it back" with L.A. in its quest for a second straight NBA title, Dec 7, 2017

Markieff Morris says he will be back for another go around with the Lakers in 2020-21.

Morris took to Twitter on Monday, writing "Run it back! #Lakeshow". He was a key reserve on L.A.'s championship squad from 2019-20, acquired for the stretch run on Feb. 23 after he was waived by the Detroit Pistons. Morris appeared in 14 regular-season games (averaging 5.3 points and 3.2 rebounds) and 21 playoff games (5.9 ppg, 3.0 rpg), providing rebounding and scoring punch for the Lakers.

Per Shams Charania of The Athletic, Morris' deal is for one year at the league minimum.
